Welcome to Highfield Stringing

Your local tennis racket stringing specialist. Professional, reliable, and fast service to keep you at the top of your game.

I use a state of the art digital tension stringing machine for the best results. I also record the Dynamic Tension, measured on the racket string bed, before and after I string so that you have a record of the difference and can check the tension loss over time.

I keep a stock of the strings below but can order any custom choices you would like:

  • Babolat RPM Blast (Black) - One of the best selling strings on the market. It is constructed from a high density co-polyester and has a cross linked silicone coating giving an octagonal structure for maximum spin. RPM Blast provides a unique feel, offering extraordinary top spin and a fast, powerful response. This string is the choice of Rafael Nadal and Jo-Wilfried Tsonga. Suitable for All Players
  • Luxlilon Big Banger AluPower (Lime or Black) - Number 1 string on the ATP Tour. This Luxilon string is for players who want more grip and bite on the ball for extra spin and control. Recommended for: Intermediate/Advanced Players
  • Solinco X-Natural (Black) - Next generation high-performance and arm-friendly multifilament string with a Diamond Rough texturing process to allow for superior performance, playability and spin generation. This Solinco string is excellent for players who a sligthly more comfortable feel on the arm while generating spin and control. Recommended for: All Players
  • Babolat Xcel Synthetic Gut (Black or BLue)- Multifilament/Polyamide synthetic gut string for comfort, feel, and power. Goes well with Luxilon Alu Power in a Hybrid String Set up. The choice of Roger Federer. Does not last as long as the strings above. Recommended for: All Players
