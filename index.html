Welcome to Highfield Stringing

Your local tennis racket stringing specialist. Professional, reliable, and fast service to keep you at the top of your game.

I use a state of the art digital tension stringing machine for the best results. I also record the Dynamic Tension, measured on the racket string bed, before and after I string to ensure quality and so that you have a record of the difference and can check the tension loss over time.

I have a range of strings in stock (see the Strings page link above) and I can order in any specific requests.

I also have a range of stencils and can create new ones if you have a special request.

Finally, I can also fit a replacement grip or overgrip to the racket and replace the grommets and bumper guard for an "as new" look.
