Grips & Grommets

Overgrips

A fresh overgrip improves comfort, control, and moisture absorption. I currently stock:

  • Head Super Comp (Black & White)
  • Yonex Super Grap (Purple)

Replacement Grips

If your main grip is worn or you want a thicker handle feel, I offer Karakal PU Super Grip (Black) replacement grips. These are tacky, durable, and widely loved for their comfort and performance.

Grommets & Bumper Guards

Grommet strips and bumper guards protect your racket frame and strings. Over time, these parts can wear down or break, especially on clay or hard courts. I offer grommet replacement services to keep your racket looking and performing its best.

New grommets can help restore the original factory look of your racket — and reduce string breakage due to sharp frame edges. Grommet replacement typically costs around £12.
